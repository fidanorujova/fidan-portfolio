Wait a minute… who are you?

My name is Fidan Orujova. I was born on December 8, 2007, in Baku, Azerbaijan. From 2014 to 2022, I studied at Secondary School No. 212 in the Narimanov district. After passing entrance exams, I was admitted in 2022 to the Academic Lyceum named after Zarifa Aliyeva, where I graduated in 2025. Currently, I am a first-year student at ADA University, majoring in Information Technology. I have intermediate-level knowledge of C++ and Python, and I am also highly confident in HTML and CSS. I enjoy building things with code and constantly improving my technical and creative skills.

Hobbies

Traveling is one of my greatest passions. I want to dedicate my life to exploring new places, learning new things, and meeting new people. I enjoy taking risks and dream of trying all kinds of extreme activities. I love listening to music — whether I'm walking or studying. I also enjoy playing chess; when I play, I imagine myself in a real-life battle where I am the queen. Lastly, swimming is another hobby that brings me peace and energy.
